## 🤖 What is RoboSoccer?

RoboSoccer is a competitive robotics event where teams design and build **manual or semi-autonomous bots** to play a soccer-style game.

Each team typically deploys:
- ⚔️ **Attacker Bot** — focused on ball control and scoring  
- 🛡️ **Defender Bot** — focused on blocking and positioning  

The competition emphasizes:
- Mechanical robustness  
- High traction and control  
- Real-time strategy and coordination  
- Reliability under physical gameplay
